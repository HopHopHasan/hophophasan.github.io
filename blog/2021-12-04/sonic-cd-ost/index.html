Sonic CD's Two OSTs
2021-12-04 | analysis games music

Ahhhh Sonic CD, a game I actually played when I was pretty young because I think it was free on PS3 or something similar.

The fact that it has two soundtracks, and four different states of levels was interesting. As a kid I didn't really know you had to get a "good future" but I still knew how to travel to the past and future. Recently I've rediscovered these soundtracks which have been rooted in the back of my mind for years ^-^

Being in the UK, we got the JP/EU (Japanese/Europe) OST instead of the US (North America) OST. Let's compare them.
